⚠ Before signing in, switch the iOS app to Staging

Make sure the MedSim iOS app is set to the Staging environment before you sign in, or your account will not be found.

How to switch environments:

  1. Open the MedSim iOS app.
  2. Long press "Env" on the Sign In screen.
  3. Open Environment.
  4. Select Staging.
  5. Return to Sign In and use the account you just created.

If the app is set to Production, this account will not be found.
