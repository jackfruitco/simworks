Before signing in, switch the iOS app to Staging
========================================
Make sure the MedSim iOS app is set to the Staging environment before
you sign in, or your account will not be found.

How to switch environments:
  1. Open the MedSim iOS app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open Environment.
  4. Select Staging.
  5. Sign in with your new account.

If the app is set to Production, this account will not be found.
